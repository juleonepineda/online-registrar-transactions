Digital Registrar Service - Step-by-Step Guide

This guide explains the simple process to request and receive official university documents online. This system eliminates the need for physical queuing at the administration building.

The 4-Step Document Request Process

  1. Log In: Access the portal using your credentials on the Home page.
  2. Verify Identity: On the Request Documents page, enter your Student ID Number and Registered BPSU Email to authenticate your identity against the centralized database.
  3. Submit Request & Pay: Select the required document (e.g., Report of Grades, Certificate of Registration) and your preferred digital payment method (E-Wallet or Bank Transfer). A payment link will be sent to your email.
  4. Receive Document: Upon successful payment and processing, the approved official document will be sent directly to your **registered BPSU email** address.

System Benefits (Objectives)

  • Reduced Waiting Time: Online verification of student records eliminates manual queuing.
  • Enhanced Convenience: Integrated digital payment (e-wallets, bank transfers) provides a cashless option.
  • Improved Accessibility: The system is available 24/7, outside of regular office hours.
  • Fast Delivery: Requested documents are delivered straight to your email.
  • Minimize Onsite Transactions: Saves time and effort for both students and staff.

⚠️ Important Considerations (Limitations)

The system is dependent on a stable internet connection; interruptions can cause delays in transactions.

There is a chance of technical issues (downtime, slow loading, program errors) which may frustrate students needing urgent documents.

Not all students have access to digital payment methods or are fully comfortable with using online platforms, which may still require the registrar's assistance.

The storage of student data raises data privacy and security concerns. The university is committed to proper security measures to prevent hacking or data leakage.
