Welcome to Your Digital Student Portal

This platform modernizes your student transactions, allowing you to handle official document requests and payments without visiting the administration building. We aim to eliminate long queues and dramatically reduce processing time.

System Features

  • Remote Request: Submit requests anytime, outside of regular office hours.
  • Efficient Processing: Secure verification via Student ID and database lookup.
  • Digital Payment: Integrated options for digital payments (e-wallets, bank transfers).
  • Fast Delivery: Approved documents are sent directly to your registered BPSU email.

Click on Request Documents above to start a transaction or view the System Guide for the 4-step process.
